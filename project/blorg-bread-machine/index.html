Blorg Bread Baker

The hand-crafted Blorg Bread Baker™ enhances any kitchen. Produced from 100% post-consumer recycled materials you’ll never once think… wait a minute, I’m baking my bread in other people’s refuse.

Features

  • Bakes artisanal bread
  • Slices
  • Doubles as a bread-box
  • Controllable-Anywhere, part of the Internet Of Things

Some versions may include a BetaMax™ video tape interface.
